Vision

We, at NGO ‘We Listen’ (registration process underway), envision a safe space for adolescents and young people, where they are listened to and are addressed about their feelings. Our vision is to create an atmosphere where mental health is not considered a taboo and therapy procedures are easily accessible to all.

Campaigns

#WeKnow

A series of online creatives posted on all our social media platforms creating awareness on mental health, the myths associated with them, the issues & problems, their types, causes, and the grounding techniques. To date, we have covered the mental issues of anxiety and anxiety disorder, focusing on what these issues are about and their grounding techniques. In the upcoming creatives, we will touch on other mental health issues like stress, depression, etc.

#WeQuote

A series of weekly online posts in which motivational & inspirational quotes written either by famous personalities or contributed to us by our audience, revolving around mental health, are restated by us.

#WeCare

Campaign initiated by NGO- We Listen, under which numerous activities are organized and planned, keeping in mind the current situations and scenarios in our country, addressing mental health issues revolving around these times.

#WeInspire

Series of motivational quotes taken from famous movies, TV series, songs, comics and novels. The youngsters can relate to the characters and quotes that inspire and motivate them. The series focuses on bringing out the happy and feel good creatives for the audience.

#TheCovidCampaign

We Listen is releasing a number of online creatives, organising activities and webinars revolving around different aspects of the CoVID-19 and lockdown situation with respect to mental health and well being.

TALE

We are reproducing the journeys of people who had been through some mental health issues in the past or are still making their way through and spread their words through videos to be posted on our online platforms. The motivation behind this initiative is to encourage people to give and seek help, open up, and break the stigma associated with mental health in our society.

We Listen Forum

We Listen forum is an online platform that connects you to the professionals on-board with us. On this forum, you can anonymously share your feelings & thoughts or can post your queries & doubts regarding mental health. The forum is active on the social media platform “Facebook”.

MythBusters

Through MythBusters, NGO- We Listen is working towards breaking the stigma towards mental health and the issues prevalent in society. Through creatives posted online, we’re busting the common myths around mental health issues and their treatment - an initiative to normalise the therapies and medications associated with mental health.

Our Collaborators

Konsciously

Konsciously provides its users with a series of interactive and transformational journeys that contain concepts derived from the fields of cognitive psychology, philosophy, and spirituality. They are designed to help users facing emotional distress to achieve holistic well-being.
Through this collaboration, We Listen and Konsciously would be working towards achieving the shared aims of helping individuals attain psychological resilience and emotional well being.

       

Wysa

We collaborated with Wysa, an AI chat that makes you feel heard and supports you in difficult times. Wysa has helped over a million people last year and we too hope to help numerous people through the collaboration.
