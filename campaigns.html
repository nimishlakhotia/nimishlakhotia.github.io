Ongoing Campaigns



#WeKnow

A series of online creatives posted on all our social media platforms creating awareness on mental health, the myths associated with them, the issues & problems, their types, causes, and the grounding techniques. To date, we have covered the mental issues of anxiety and anxiety disorder, focusing on what these issues are about and their grounding techniques. In the upcoming creatives, we will touch on other mental health issues like stress, depression, etc.

#WeQuote

A series of weekly online posts in which motivational & inspirational quotes written either by famous personalities or contributed to us by our audience, revolving around mental health, are restated by us.

#WeCare

Campaign initiated by NGO- We Listen, under which numerous activities are organized and planned, keeping in mind the current situations and scenarios in our country, addressing mental health issues revolving around these times.

#WeInspire

Series of motivational quotes taken from famous movies, TV series, songs, comics and novels. The youngsters can relate to the characters and quotes that inspire and motivate them. The series focuses on bringing out the happy and feel good creatives for the audience.

#TheCovidCampaign

We Listen is releasing a number of online creatives, organising activities and webinars revolving around different aspects of the CoVID-19 and lockdown situation with respect to mental health and well being.

TALE

We are reproducing the journeys of people who had been through some mental health issues in the past or are still making their way through and spread their words through videos to be posted on our online platforms. The motivation behind this initiative is to encourage people to give and seek help, open up, and break the stigma associated with mental health in our society.

We Listen Forum

We Listen forum is an online platform that connects you to the professionals on-board with us. On this forum, you can anonymously share your feelings & thoughts or can post your queries & doubts regarding mental health. The forum is active on the social media platform “Facebook”.

MythBusters

Through MythBusters, NGO- We Listen is working towards breaking the stigma towards mental health and the issues prevalent in society. Through creatives posted online, we’re busting the common myths around mental health issues and their treatment - an initiative to normalise the therapies and medications associated with mental health.

Surveys

The survey team is working on developing surveys for parents of pre-teens (9-12 years old) and interviewing the parents & children to get a fair idea about the issue of high suicide rate in India due to rough family relationships. The survey will study how aware parents are about their children’s mental and emotional health. It will then be used to develop products that will help parents to improve their supervision wrt the mental health needs of their children.

NGO Studies

The team is thoroughly studying about the different organisations related to mental health in India, US, UK and Australia. This study will help us to better understand the working and functioning of these organisations, few of which we can incorporate in our NGO in the future.

Health Care Documentation

The team is currently working on documents on “Mental Health: Medicines & Academics”, focusing on the different categories of medicines used in mental health treatments, studying about their side effects, market prices, and caregiver support. It will also contain a module on mental health counseling programs running in various schools, suggesting various reforms, improvements, and scope for the future.

Research & Project Series

This project, as of now, is headed by the Mental Health Coach and Cognitive Behavioural Therapist, Karialetter. The freelance project will have various research topics with a certain number of volunteers working under a mentor for the projects on different aspects of mental health. We’re currently working to involve more professionals to mentor the projects to be launched soon.

Emotional Crisis Helpline

Many times help may not reach out to us immediately, even making an appointment with a mental health counselor may take time. To provide effective solutions to such urgent cases, we are aiming to provide hotline counseling to one and all - a safe space where anyone can easily talk about their feelings, emotional pain, and distress without any constraints and provide basic help on the spot.

Learning & Development Sessions

At We Listen, we truly believe that nurturing and investing in the growth of our volunteers is the best way to succeed as an organization. Hence, the learning and development sessions are organised where the volunteers and interns lead a discussion educating the others in the team about various topics. We encourage all our volunteers and interns, especially all research associates, to attend these sessions.

Future Campaigns



Online Support Group | Discord

The Discord Forum is intended to create a safe space and a sense of community, encouraging discussions on various sensitive issues, while the Podcast will help bring awareness about different mental health issues and give a sense of support to the community.

MenTALK Campaign

The campaign centers around the mental health problems and stigma faced by men. Through this campaign, we fight the misconceptions of men being “emotionless” or “emotionally strongly”. Various sessions and activities are being planned under this campaign.

Suicide Prevention Campaign

The campaign focuses on the issue of suicides due to mental health problems not being addressed or treated on time. NGO- We Listen plans to provide the required education and support to the people going through a difficult time. Various sessions, activities and creatives are being planned as a part of this campaign.

Past Campaigns



Mental Health Month

We organized events spanning the entire month with the goal to educate people, fight the stigma and provide support related to mental health and the mental health issues.

Webinar Series

We collaborated with established Mental Health Experts, and conducted informative sessions on topics including, “The Role of Nutrition on Mental Health” by the Health Coach and Vegan Chef Rosina Alessi and “Impact of Pets on Mental Health” by the animal-assisted therapist and neuropsychology research scholar Unnati Hunjan.

CBT Series

This series consisted of 3 module-based sessions to be headed by the Mental Health & Wellness Coach - Sarah Baxter. These sessions were designed for anyone and everyone who had an interest in understanding themselves better.

Giveaway Coaching Program

We understand how difficult it can be for someone to handle some situations while facing anxiety, depression, or any other mental health issues. We launched this program giving a chance to avail of a 3-month Coaching Program to be headed by the Health Coach Rosina Alessi.

Yoga for your Spirits

We conducted a session, in collaboration with Master Wholistic Coach Mr. Yogi Sakha on the International Yoga Day, which talked about how Yoga has been recognized as a powerful tool that assists one in mental health, meditation, relaxation, and well being followed by some meditation exercises.

Suicide Awareness & Prevention

NGO- We Listen organized a session on “Suicide Awareness & Prevention” on 1st July 2020 conducted by the Mental Health & Wellness Coach Sarah Baxter. This session talked about how all things that seem difficult may come out to be very easy.

Pride Month

We released an online creative by Richa Vashista (she/her), a queer affirmative mental health professional, who shares how we can help the LGBTQIA+ community, to create a better and supportive environment for them.
