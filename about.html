Mission

Our mission is to create awareness, break the stigma, and lend support to those suffering from mental health problems. The idea is to develop and provide for a virtual space where people can talk about their feelings, emotional pain, and distress without any constraints.

We also plan to connect the concerned person with a professional where needed. We plan to push for the social acceptance of people with mental health issues. In the longer run, we aim to fulfill this through sustainable models of care that appropriately address the elements of mental health.
